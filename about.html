About Us

Built-Mech Services obtained its legal prerogative to commence business in MVAC field as far back as 1994. We were the minnows then, to join the cluster of giants in the trade backed by several years of business experience. We started our business with only 03 persons and we look back with joy and reminiscence that these 3 persons are still in the active service of the company for the last 26 years.
Our journey was not rosy and our sheer determination and self-confidence brought us where we are today. The business world was turning fast and we had to keep pace, otherwise we would have been thrown to a way side long time back. Now we are studded with technical personnel with high caliber, fleet of vehicles for transport needs, inter active communication system, administrators with above average knowledge and experience who could be entrusted with any project of any magnitude. Giant AC project, Lanka Hospitals, Central Bank of Sri Lanka, Benthota Beach Hotel, MAS Holdings among others trail behind us as land marks of our reputation in this field. Projects we have completed overseas in Bangladesh, Vietnam and Qatar confirm our versatility and our adaptability to conditions local and overseas.
We have proudly trumped over incredible odds using extraordinary strength and stability. We navigated the rough waters of global economy and by standing true to its valued ethos and maintained standards of quality and efficiency at all times. As a long player in the industry, Built-Mech Services realized the importance of comport, durability and bounteous technique introduced through materials and equipment to satisfy customer satisfaction and needs.
We are proud authorized dealer for world top brand air conditioners viz. “Carrier” “Midea” “Dunhambush” “ Toshiba” “ Hitachi” “Kingsun” enabling us to satisfy any customer needs. Also, we are ISO certified and enjoy electro Mechanical Grade One granted by Sri Lanka Government sponsored Construction Industry Development Authority as a bench mark of qualification acceptable for awarding any project.
We now look forward to reach the epitome of undisputed leadership in the air conditioning industry in Sri Lanka.

Vision | Mission

Vision

To be the undisputed leader in mechanical ventilation and air conditioning (MVAC) industry in Sri Lanka

Mission

To be a leading service provider in the air conditioning field in Sri Lanka and overseas.

To develop and retain a highly motivated team of employees who recognize the ethical and professional standards of a demanding service sector.

WHY CHOOSE US?

Our company is blessed with the most essential pre requisites to be a cynosure of a service provider in air conditioning and ventilation systems. Punctuality, experience, technical performance and excellent maintenance program are the bench marks attached to our forte.

We at Built-Mech services have a proven track record being in the industry for over quarter century having incorporated in1994. Our clear understanding of the needs of the would be customers and the flexibility to adapt service deliveries to fulfill their needs with innovative amends where necessary won us the day to be a front line air conditioning company

Our success story was accelerated in 7 by being appointed as an authorized dealer for world renowned “Carrier” air conditioning equipment in Sri Lanka. Thereafter, we were appointed as authorized dealers for more brands “Dunham Bush” “Toshiba” “Hitachi” and “Midea”. Facilitating us to offer tailor made proposals to our customers depending on their financial capabilities.

As we are penetrating all avenues to keep abreast with latest technologies, we strove hard to get ISO 9001:2008 in 2009 certification from SGS United Kingdom as far back as 2009. Another prestigious achievement is the granting of EM Grade One by the Construction industry Development Authority of Sri Lanka stamping our technical expertise to handle any installation of any magnitude.
